### manual disk: 
- **2 partitions**
- A  `1GB boot  (mount: '/boot', bootable flag on,)`
- B  `remaining space, ext4. `
- then do `config lvm`
  -  v group: `vg1`
  -  logical vols in `vg1`:
    - lswap (use as swap, 4GB)
    - lroot (`40GB`,  use as ext4, mount `/root/`)
    - lhome (`remaining space`,  use as ext4, mount `/home/`)
